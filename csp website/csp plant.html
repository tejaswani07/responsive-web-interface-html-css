HOW CAN WE PREPARE MANURE

Composting:

1:Composting is a disposal method of kitchen waste.

2:Kitchen wastes are biodegradable and can convert to manure.

3:This manure can be used for growing plants.

4:The conversion of biodegradable wastes into manure is called composting.

Steps involving composting:

STEP1:Dig a pit that is 30 cm deep in a place where direct sunlight is not available.

STEP2:Spread a 2cm thick layer of sand on the floor of the pit.

STEP3:Put garbage over the sand layer.

STEP4:Also kept cow dung and newspaper in the pit.

STEP5:Sprinkle some water on it.

STEP6:Put red worms on the pit.

STEP7:Cover the pit with a gunny bag or a layer of grass.

STEP8:The redworms will feed the kitchen waste and their excreta is collected in the pit.

STEP9:These excreta is collected after 3-4 weeks and this soil-like loose material is called manure.
